pkgcore.spawn.spawn(mycommand, env=None, executable=None, fd_pipes=None, returnpid=False, uid=None, gid=None, groups=None, umask=None, logfile=None, cwd=None)[source]¶

wrapper around execve

Parameters:
  • executable – controls what the process is named (what it would show up as under top for example)
  • fd_pipes (mapping from existing fd to fd (inside the new process)) – controls what fd’s are left open in the spawned process-
  • returnpid – controls whether spawn waits for the process to finish, or returns the pid.
